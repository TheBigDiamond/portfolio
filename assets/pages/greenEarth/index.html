About us:

GreenEarth is a non-profit organization that aims to preserve the planet and it's inhabitants. Our biggest projects include: Greenify, Nature Guard and Eco Solutions.

Our projects:

Greenify: This project is aimed to plant as much green life as possible on steril land, so there could be more life around the world but also to create more oxygen and prevent pollution.

Nature Guard: This project is all about preserving the life that we ourselves create on this planet. With just a small donation, you can contribute too by helping protect the wildlife!

Eco Solutions: This project is our biggest and proudest yet! We installed eco-friendly stations around the world where you can rent out an e-scooter and explore the beauty of this world
